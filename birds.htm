Birds in Cedars Park

Many bird species have been observed in Cedars Park. Naturally, they come and go, however on any visit to the park a wide variety of different birds can be seen, since the area provides a quiet "green space" with plenty of trees, making a perfect habitat, in stark contrast to the busy urban areas around the park, and many on these list are permanently resident in the park.
