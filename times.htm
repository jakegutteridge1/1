Closing Times

Cedars Park is open every day of the year except Christmas Day. The gates are opened at 07:00 daily, and closing times vary as follows: 

January		16:00
February	17:00
March	  	18:00
April	  	19:00
May   		20:00
June	  	21:00
July	  	21:00
August		20:30
September	19:30
October		18:00
November	16:30
December	16:00

Please note that the pedestrian gates are locked 15 minutes before closing time.
