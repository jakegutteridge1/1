Community Involvement at Cedars Park

There are several ways in which you can contribute towards the maintenance and development of Cedars Park.

Volunteering

A great way to maintain physical and mental health, make new friends, and improve Cedars Park is to help out at one of the park's regular volunteering activities.

The Friends of Cedars Park is a volunteer group suited to all ages. Membership is free. If you would like to get involved with the Friends of Cedars Park, please see the group's Facebook page for the latest opportunities.

Cheshunt Extended Services (ChExS) arrange events and activities to support the local community, including gardening activities, and an annual Grow Club competition for local schools which includes a public fun day, often held at Cedars Park. Please see their Facebook page.

Website

Support this website by adding new pages, suggesting changes to existing pages, or contributing photos.

Forum

Since 2019, the park has an internet forum, where you can share your experiences and photos of Cedars Park, or discuss matters online with other visitors to the park.
