Great War Centennial Corner

This is Cedars Park's War Memorial, which is primarily in memory of the Fallen in the First World War, however it also commemorates and respects those lost in other conflicts.

The two main parts of this Memorial are the WWI replica tank and Tommy Statue, which may be expanded upon below, however there is also a Centennial Bench made of steel, with imagery of poppies and soldiers walking to war. The bench was produced by David Ogilvie Engineering, and several benches with this design can be found across the country.

A remembrance service is held at this war memorial every Armistice Day. Photographs of past services have been compiled here.
