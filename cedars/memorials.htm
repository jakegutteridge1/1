Personal Memorials in Cedars Park

Many people choose to remember a relative who has passed away by having a memorial bench or tree planted in Cedars Park. A list of these memorials has been compiled by Tarant Hobbs and Jake Gutteridge. Information is wanted for those whose names are not listed, i.e. blank sections and when there is only a memorial number and no name. If you are a relative of a person remembered in Cedars Park whose name is not listed here, please contact us by e-mailing jakant@mail.com. This will help in creating a comprehensive list of the personal memorials.

The list may be viewed here.

There is also a Google Map of these memorials, showing photos and their locations in the Park, which is currently being produced as a community project. This may be viewed here.
