Cedars Park

Theobalds Lane, Cheshunt, Hertfordshire, EN8 8LS, England
Cedars Park is a public park covering around 40 acres. As the former site of Theobalds Palace, a significant royal estate, it is of great historical and national interest.

The park was opened on 2 July 1921 and has been expanded over the years. A mature and varied arboretum covers the park, and there is a mixture of modern features and remaining palace structures.
