Jakant Research

This website covers the independent activities of Tarant Hobbs and Jake Gutteridge. We are a grandfather and grandson based in Essex and Hertfordshire respectively, who are together often and independently carry out identification and management of trees and memorials in Cedars Park in Cheshunt, Hertfordshire, as well as researching the park's history, as the grounds were formerly a significant Royal Palace. Our website also covers other topics that we have researched.

We are not a company - the name "Jakant" is simply a fusion of our first names.
