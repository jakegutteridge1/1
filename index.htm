Welcome to the Cedars Park Research Website

This site is dedicated to researching the history and features of Cedars Park in Cheshunt, and the Royal Palace of Theobalds, which stood on the grounds until it was largely destroyed in the English Civil War.

  • This is not intended to replace the main park website, cedarspark.org, which should be referred to for visiting information such as opening times and facilities.
  • For community activities and events, please visit the Friends of Cedars Park page on Facebook.
  • To speak with park management, please contact Broxbourne Council.
