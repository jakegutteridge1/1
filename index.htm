Cedars Park



Introductory Video
Cedars Park is a public park located on Theobalds Lane, Cheshunt, Hertfordshire, EN8 8RU, England. The grounds once held Theobalds Palace, a large stately home used by several very important people, most notably Queen Elizabeth I and King James I.

The public park of "The Cedars" was donated by Admiral Sir Hedworth Meux and opened on 2 July 1921. Today, it retains a charming character and peaceful atmosphere, despite being surrounded by a busy dual carriageway on two sides.

Cedars Park has been Green Flag Awarded every year since 2009.
