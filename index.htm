Welcome to Jakant Research

This website covers the independent activities of Tarant Hobbs and Jake Gutteridge. We are regular visitors (since 2006) to Cedars Park in Cheshunt, and carry out projects in the park and are continually researching its history and features to be displayed on this site. Please note that the main website for Cedars Park is cedarspark.org, and visitors wishing to find information such as opening times & facilities should visit that site.

This website was created in 2020. Pages are continually updated and new ones added. Most images on this website can be enlarged by clicking on them. In addition to the pages below, video content is available at our YouTube channel. The name "Jakant" (pronounced jay-kant) is simply a fusion of both our first names. E-mail is welcomed at jakant@mail.com.
