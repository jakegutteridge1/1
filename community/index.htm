Contribute to the Website

Community involvement with Cedars Park's Web pages

We welcome any content from the public, related to Cedars Park or Theobalds in any way. Please send this to cedarspark@mail.com. You will be credited for any content that you submit (unless specified that you do not want your name published on the website).

Contribute

  • PAGES are welcomed as HTML or TXT files, which we will directly upload to the site without alterations (other than adding your name), however alternatively you can type text into the body of your e-mail and we will format it for the Web.
  • We have a community Photo Gallery where we have compiled some of the community's PHOTOS of Cedars Park from the internet, however you can also submit your own to be displayed on the page.
  • If you have a PHYSICAL ITEM that you would like to send in, please photograph/scan this so that it can be displayed on the website.

Assist in historical research

In order to ensure that all information is accurate, we require historians to confirm the following points prior to adding onto the main sections of this page:

  • When did Major-General William Packer acquire Theobalds Palace?
  • Any information about the Cromwell family and their connection to Theobalds
  • Who was the Earl of Cavan in 1921? Rudolph Lambart?
  • Who were the "two influential Presbyterian theologians" who lived at Theobalds?
  • When did Lord Barrington, Sir Thomas Abney and Isaac Watts live at Theobalds?
  • When did Paul Hentzner and Baron Waldstein visit Theobalds?

Share your memories of Cedars Park

The park's Community Forum has hardly been used since its launch in 2019. We know that many visitors have memories and photos of Cedars Park from the distant past, therefore it would be great to share these with the community, not just for happy memories but also to show younger people what the park used to look like.
