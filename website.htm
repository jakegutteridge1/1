About This Website

Several websites for Cedars Park have existed in the past, alongside Broxbourne Council's page.

This website was created following the closure of www.cedarspark.org, which lasted 2019 to December 2021. The content for this site has been researched since 2020 by Tarant Hobbs and Jake Gutteridge, and nothing was taken from the previous one.
