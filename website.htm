About the Cedars Park website

  • This website was launched in late 2021 (other sites existed before, but they were eventually closed)
  • Volunteers manage the website, and it is frequently updated and expanded.
  • The site is independent and not associated with Broxbourne Council or the Friends of Cedars Park.
  • All content on this website* is free to distribute, providing the site is credited.
    • *Please refrain from sharing individual images, as some of these could be externally sourced.
  • Large files are held at external websites, so as not to hog bandwidth on this site.
