Geographical Terminology of Britain

UNITED KINGDOM (UK) - The united country of England, Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland.

GREAT BRITAIN (GB) - The largest island in the United Kingdom, encompassing most of England, Scotland and Wales. Sometimes used as a term of endearment for the UK by Brits (as in 'great' meaning very good, or referring to the great (in size) British Empire), therefore the name is often erroneously used to describe the whole of the UK.

ENGLAND - A country in the United Kingdom, on the island of Great Britain.

SCOTLAND - A country in the United Kingdom, on the island of Great Britain.

WALES - A country in the United Kingdom, on the island of Great Britain..

NORTHERN IRELAND - A country in the United Kingdom, on the island of Ireland.

IRELAND - An island to the West of Great Britain, encompassing the Republic of Ireland, and Northern Ireland.

REPUBLIC OF IRELAND (ROI) - A country on the island of Ireland. Part of the United Kingdom until 1922. Very often shortened to just Ireland.

BRITISH ISLES - Great Britain, Ireland and the surrounding smaller islands.

BRITAIN - Can refer to both Great Britain & the entire United Kingdom.

BRITISH EMPIRE - Countries that the Brits (mainly the English) invaded and gained political control over. Some Empire colonies retained their own government, while Britain still has control over some matters in self-governing colonies, and has absolute control over some others (Crown colonies).

COMMONWEALTH - A political association of 54 countries, most of which are former British colonies. *A full list will be added soon*

BRITISH COLONY/TERRITORY - A country that Britain colonised.

COUNTY - A geographical division of the country. There are several towns within a county. More information can be found at www.theedkins.co.uk/uklocalgov/whtsacty.htm .

BOROUGH - An area of a small number of larger towns (some just 1), governed by a Borough Council.

DISTRICT - An area of a large number of smaller towns, governed by a District Council.

* Some areas are both a Borough and a District, in which case the local government will choose to name itself either a Borough Council or a District Council.

DIOCESE - An area governed by a bishop of a major national church (e.g. Church of England).

CHURCH PARISH - A division within a diocese.

CIVIL PARISH - (in England) An area with a Parish Council, a small form of local government which usually operates alongside a larger District or Borough Council.

COMMUNITY - (in Wales) The equivalent of an English Civil Parish, although some (mostly very small) communities do not have a Community Council.

DIOCESE - An area governed by a bishop.

HAMLET - A very small settlement, without a church.

POSTCODE - An alpha-numeric code designated to a specific area, for the purpose of sorting post. They have been in use since the 1960s, however POSTAL COUNTIES were also used by the Royal Mail for post sorting from 1974 to 1996.

LONDON - Capital city of England and the whole United Kingdom.

CITY OF LONDON - A borough of London, and an English county.

GREATER LONDON - London (except the City) and some surrounding areas.

[PLACE NAME] TOWN - Can mean A) the centre of a city, B) a part of an area that is popular for partying, or C) a place before it became a busy city.
