Bocce at Cedars Park

Bocce ("bocheh") is an ancient Italian bowling game. A bocce court was added to Cedars Park in 2011 and has since been used very frequently by the local Italian community.

Playing bocce is a great way for anyone to relax, and is suited to all ages. Even if you are in a wheelchair, bocce is quite easy to play - professionally, bocce for disabled people is known as 'boccia', however it is virtually the same as standard bocce. Why not buy a set and start playing in the park?

Bocce is a simple game that is very easy to learn. We recommend this beginner's guide.
