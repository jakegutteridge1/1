Cedars Park Covenant

This document was written by Admiral of the Fleet Sir Hedworth Meux in 1919. He inherited Theobalds and donated a portion of the grounds to be opened as a public park for the people of Cheshunt. It also outlines how he intended the park to be managed and used.

Two versions of the covenant are available - a scan of the original papers from HM Land Registry, or a typed copy which is clearer to read - both contain the same content.
