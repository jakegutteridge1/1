Cedars Park Covenant

This document was written by Admiral of the Fleet the Honourable Sir Hedworth Meux in 1919. It is a legal donation of a portion of his grounds (Theobalds) to be opened as a public park for the people of Cheshunt. It also outlines how he intended the park to be managed and used.

A scan of the original papers may be found here, however it has also been typed up in a clearer version here.
