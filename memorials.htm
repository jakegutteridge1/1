Memorial Trees and Benches

in Cedars Park, Cheshunt

Apply for a Memorial Plaque
Many people choose to remember a relative or friend who has passed away by having a memorial bench or tree added to Cedars Park. In order to make it easier to find memorials, they have been listed on this page, and a Google Earth map with photos of plaques is also being produced as a community project.

The data below has been compiled by Tarant Hobbs and Jake Gutteridge since 2019. Broxbourne Council claim to hold their own memorial list, however this is not publicly accessible and we have been unable to access it on request, hence the creation of this independent listing for public reference.

Can You Help?

We are seeking information about those remembered against numbers at the base of memorial trees that do not include name plaques. If you can help, kindly advise us by e-mailing cedarspark@mail.com, so that a comprehensive list may be produced.

Note

  • Memorials are sorted by the 'zone' of Cedars Park in which they are located. These are shown on the Zone Map.
  • An ID number has been allocated (M __) where names cannot be included, such as when a plaque is missing or illegible.
  • We are in the process of copying the contents of plaques (rather than names only), which will be shown in italic.
  • Last Updated: 1 May 2022

Trees

The following codes are used to differentiate memorial plaque types, and may be used in combination.
  • cm = concrete mould (l = loose, s = sunken)
  • spo = stone post
  • wpo = wooden post
  • mpo = metal post
  • spl = stone plate
  • wpo = wooden post
  • mpl = metal plate
  • wpl = wooden plate
  • ppl = plastic plate
  • x = no memorial marker (plate or post)
  • xpl = no plate (empty post)
  • xt = no tree
