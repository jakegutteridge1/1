Platinum Jubilee "Street Party" and Rock Performance at Cedars Park

Sunday 5 June 2022

The Friends of Cedars Park organised a picnic at the Great War Centennial Corner to celebrate Queen Elizabeth II's Platinum Jubilee and thank supporters of the group, including the Mayor, Cllr Siobhan Monaghan, and Tesco Community Champions, as well as some other local people. Food and drinks were prepared by the Cedars Park cafe. The public were invited to join with their picnic blankets and tables, however no one showed up, perhaps due to the wet weather on the day! The SuperVillain band delivered an excellent performance of a selection of rock songs from 2.45PM to 4.15PM.
