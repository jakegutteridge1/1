Concerns about the management of Cedars Park

Video from 28 August 2021

We have observed continual failings in the management of Cedars Park and are concerned that Broxbourne Borough Council have failed to pay attention to, and provide sufficient action on, several ongoing issues in the park. Please watch the video to understand some of these problems.

Please e-mail us at cedarspark@mail.com to show your agreement in the opinion that the council are managing Cedars Park with shortcomings, and that significant improvement is essential.
