The problem with Cedars Park's palace model plinths

The excellent plinths with models of Theobalds Palace were added to Cedars Park as part of the Heritage Lottery Fund improvements to the park. It is great to have these plinths in the park, and they depict different areas of the palace with information plaques, which include Braille, however the plinths were poorly designed, and some of the concrete supports are now in a concerning condition. Please watch the videos for more information.

Please e-mail cedarspark@mail.com if you agree that Broxbourne Council should add PMMA sheeting on top of all model plinths in Cedars Park, so that water and detritus do not collect, and if you agree that some of the concrete supports should be replaced or filled.
