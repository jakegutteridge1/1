Facilities (Features at index page)

  • Fully wheelchair accessible
  • Toilets (including disabled) and baby changing
  • Cafe
  • Meeting room - this may be hired through the cafe.
The following is strictly forbidden in Cedars Park:
  • Damaging trees/branches.
  • Picking flowers/plants.
  • Harassing wildlife.
  • Littering.
  • Drawing graffiti anywhere in the park.
  • Riding bicycles/scooters on the bocce court, or digging the court surface
  • Pulling up turf from the Venusberg mound.

These are simple rules that the vast majority of the park community follow without issue, however due to past events, we feel that it is necessary to have them listed on this site. It is the duty of parents to ensure that their children behave sensibly in the park.

Also, please see the Canine Code for important details about dogs.
