  • Small function room, which may be hired through the cafe.
(Features may be found at the index page.)

The following is strictly forbidden in Cedars Park:
  • Damaging trees/branches.
  • Picking flowers/plants.
  • Harassing wildlife.
  • Littering.
  • Drawing graffiti anywhere in the park.
  • Riding bicycles/scooters on the bocce court, or digging the court surface
  • Pulling up turf from the Venusberg mound.

These simple and obvious rules are followed by the majority of the park community without issue, however due to past events, we feel that it is necessary to have them listed on this site. It is the duty of parents to ensure that their children behave sensibly in the park. Cedars Park is a historic site and must be treated with respect.

Also, please see the Canine Code for important details about dogs.
