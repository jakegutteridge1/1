A Message From The Webmaster

W e l c o m e

Thank you for visiting the Cedars Park website, which I took over at the turn of 2021/22, creating this new one with my grandfather Tarant Hobbs, who has specifically researched the history of the Great War Centennial Corner. We have worked together to tag the trees and list the memorials around the park, and we both manage the data collected through those projects. We also have a personal website, Jakant Research.

The intention for this site is to provide a simple way for people to find information about Cedars Park, while highlighting the park's rich history as the former site of Theobalds Palace, a royal residence where significant historical events have taken place. Many people (especially non-local visitors) do not realise the importance of this parkland.

My grandfather and I both work to encourage improvements in the care and preservation of the park, and bring attention to any failings in its management, so that it may be continually improved and retain its status as the premier park in the Broxbourne Borough.

This website is in continual development and any comments are appreciated by e-mailing us at cedarspark@mail.com. You are also welcome to submit any information, pages or images to be displayed on the site.

Note

My grandfather supports me in many ways, and is eager to contribute to this website where he can, however I have created most of the pages on this site, and manage it, hence I have only listed myself as the "Webmaster".

We created our original website together, which was called "Jakant Research" and covered some of Cedars Park plus other topics, and when the previous park website (www.cedarspark.org) shut down, we migrated relevant pages to this address (and expanded them) and separated other topics to a personal site.

This website has always been a joint effort between both of us, and we continue to improve it together!

Jake Gutteridge, June 2022
